EGA light wallet

Phone / tablet friendly shell. Connects to your node RPC (not public internet). Scaffold for accessibility — not a custodian.

—
Immature rewards need confirmations before they spend.
—
Security: Only use RPC on localhost or a trusted VPN/Tailscale to your home node. Never put your rpcpassword on a public website host. Production mobile app will use a light server protocol — this page is the first step for “poor device” access.

Mining on Android (Yespower/RandomX) will ship as a separate worker app pointing at pool or solo.
